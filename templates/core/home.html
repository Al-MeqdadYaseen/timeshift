⏱️ Welcome to Timeshift

Timeshift is a simple interactive tool to explore one of the most fascinating predictions of Einstein's theory of relativity: time dilation.

⚡Relativitistic

When objects move at speeds close to light, time slows down. The faster you go, the slower time passes for you relative to a stationary observer.

Try Calculator →

🌍 Gravitational Time Dilation

Strong gravity also slows time. Near massive objects like neutron stars or black holes, time runs significantly slower than observed from a distance.

Try Calculator →

✨ Both effects are real and measured. GPS satellites must correct for both relativistic and gravitational time dilation to give accurate positioning!
